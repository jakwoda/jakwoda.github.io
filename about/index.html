about

 


My practice as a fluid-medium designer drifts between visual, audible, and sensory experiences.

Drawing inspiration from the properties of water, both material and philosophical, I approach creation as an evolving process rather than a fixed outcome. Looking through the liquid lens, I work with media that flow across boundaries: from video and code to objects, workshops, and printed matter. My work spans interactive and generative media, installations, and participatory formats that invite audiences to slow down, sense, and make something together.

Rooted in everyday primitive gestures-like drawing, skateboarding, cooking, or photography-each project becomes a tool for perceiving the world differently. I translate technology into something approachable, tactile, shared, and alive.

 



Jak Woda 2026
